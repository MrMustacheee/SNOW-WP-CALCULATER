How this works

  1. Upload your bid drawing — PDF or image, top-left button.
  2. Set your scale. If it's a PDF with a printed ratio like "1:50" in the title block, use the box at the top of the Setup tab — type the number, click Apply, done. Otherwise click Set Scale in the toolbar and click both ends of any labeled dimension on the drawing.
  3. Double-check it. Click ✓ Verify Scale and click across a different labeled dimension. It'll tell you if it matches or offer to fix it.
  4. Trace your walls. Click + Wall, then click along the wall, corner to corner. Click ✓ Finish (in the bar that appears), press Enter, or double-click to end that wall run. Hold Shift while clicking to force a perfectly straight line. Click ✕ Stop when you're done tracing walls entirely.
  5. Mark doors/windows. Click + Opening, then click the two opposite corners of the opening — that area gets subtracted automatically.
  6. Watch "Rolls needed" update live in the top bar. Open the Results tab for the full breakdown by wall and by group.
  7. Group rooms. Type the same name (like "WC A") into a wall's Group box in the Walls tab to get subtotals per room in Results.
  8. Leave notes. Click 📍 Note to pin a comment anywhere on the drawing — find every note later in the Log tab.

Tip: press Esc anytime to stop whatever tool you're using. Every tool also has a ✕ Stop button in the bar under the toolbar while it's active.
